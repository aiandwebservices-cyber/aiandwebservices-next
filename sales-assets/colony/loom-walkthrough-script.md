# Loom Walkthrough Script - 90 seconds

Target: ~90 seconds. Demo cohort (Smith Family Dental) loaded.

## 0:00–0:10 - Hook
"I'm David. I built this dashboard for my small business customers. Here's what they see when they log in."
- show home screen, feed sliding up

## 0:10–0:25 - The crew
"Every customer gets 8 AI agents working for them."
- hover over Bot Roster. Click Bill Nye.

"This is Bill Nye, the data scientist. Every Sunday he analyzes the week's leads and drops a report. Here's what he found this week."
- scroll through Bill Nye's profile panel, close it

## 0:25–0:45 - The feed
"The feed shows the agents actually working. New lead came in 2 hours ago. Another 2 hours ago. Bill Nye ran overnight."
- click a recent HOT lead event

"Click any lead and you see the drafted outreach email, the business signals that put them on our radar, and one-click mark contacted."
- show lead detail panel scrolled through

## 0:45–1:05 - Revenue moves
"On the right side is what needs your attention today. HOT leads aging, proposals pending. This is the 'what do I do next' screen."
- close lead panel, highlight revenue moves column

## 1:05–1:20 - Inbox
"Every lead lives in the Inbox. Filter by temperature, niche, source. Everything searchable."
- navigate to /colony/inbox, click a filter

## 1:20–1:30 - Close
"Colony is included with Revenue Engine and AI-First plans. Link in the description. If you're a small business owner losing leads, let's talk."
- back to home, end on feed



## Recording tips
- Don't talk over clicks, let the animations play
- Keep mouse movements smooth (practice once without recording)
- If you stumble, restart the whole take, it's easier than editing
- Open Colony in dark mode before recording
- Hide your browser bookmarks bar
