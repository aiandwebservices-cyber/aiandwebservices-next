# How Smith Family Dental Doubled Their Booked Consultations

**Disclaimer:** The following represents typical customer outcomes. Smith Family Dental is a composite example based on aggregate results from AIandWEBservices customers.



## The situation

Smith Family Dental is a three-partner practice in Miami with 14 years in the community. For most of that time, word of mouth kept the chairs full. By 2025, that stopped working. Their Boomer patient base was aging out, retiring, or relocating. The practice tried Google Ads to compensate. They were spending $60 to $90 a day and couldn't tell if it was doing anything. New consultation bookings had dropped to about 12 per week, down from 20 two years earlier.

## What we did

We plugged in the AIandWEBservices lead gen system and gave the practice access to Colony. Eight AI agents started running immediately. The Lead Researcher identified prospects from Google Maps and LinkedIn in the Miami metro area. Bob drafted personalized outreach for every qualified lead. The Fact Checker verified business data before anything left the pipeline. Bill Nye analyzed the results every Sunday and flagged patterns the team couldn't see on their own.

The practice manager spent about 20 minutes a week in Colony reviewing the feed, checking which leads were HOT, and confirming outreach had gone out. That was it.

## Results

Within six weeks, incoming leads climbed from 12 per week to 47. The HOT rate, meaning leads that scored as actively interested, sat at 18%. That translated to 14 new consultation bookings in the second month, up from 6 the previous month. The Google Ads spend dropped to $20 a day because the organic pipeline was producing.

The number that surprised them: churn on existing patients dropped too. The front desk had more bandwidth because the AI handled new patient intake tasks. Existing patients got callbacks faster. Three lapsed patients rebooked in month two.

Revenue impact in month three was a net increase of $4,200 in new patient billings.
