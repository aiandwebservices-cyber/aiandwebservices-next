**Your AI workforce, finally visible.**

## What it does

Colony is a live operations dashboard included with Revenue Engine and AI-First plans. Eight AI agents run your lead generation pipeline around the clock. Colony shows you everything they're doing in real time: new leads, outreach drafted, deals moving, and weekly analysis from your AI data scientist. You open it Monday morning and the work is already done.

![Bot Roster view](/colony-screenshots/bots.png)

The activity feed tracks every action your agents take. A new prospect surfaces in Miami. Bob drafts the outreach email. Fact Checker verifies the business details. The Scheduler runs the full pipeline three times a day so nothing sits idle overnight.

![Revenue moves panel](/colony-screenshots/revenue.png)

The revenue moves panel shows what needs your attention today. HOT leads aging without contact. Proposals waiting. Deals that haven't moved in a week. It's the "what do I do right now" screen.

## Who it's for

Colony is built for small business owners with 1 to 20 employees who want AI lead gen that's actually accountable. If you've tried automation tools before and ended up with a black box you couldn't trust, Colony is the opposite of that. You see the agents, the decisions, and the results in one place.
