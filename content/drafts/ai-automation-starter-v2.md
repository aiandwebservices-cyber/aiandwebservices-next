## 7. NEW: Page-Specific FAQ (5–7 questions)

**Header:** "Questions About AI Automation Starter"

**Q1: "Won't prospects be annoyed talking to a bot?"**
- A: Not if it's polite and actually answers their questions. The AI is trained to be helpful, not robotic. Most prospects don't mind—they get an instant answer instead of waiting 6 hours. The AI's only job is to answer FAQs and qualify. For real conversations, it hands off to you.

**Q2: "What if the AI gives a wrong answer?"**
- A: That's why David monitors it for 30 days after launch and continues monthly optimization. If an answer is wrong, he retrains the AI. Plus, every conversation is logged, so you can see what the AI said and correct it. We also set up a "escalation" button so if a prospect isn't satisfied, they can request you directly.

**Q3: "Can the AI handle complex questions?"**
- A: It can handle 60–80% of inquiries (FAQs, basic qualify questions, booking). For complex questions ("Can you do X custom thing?" or "How would you approach Y?"), the AI detects that it doesn't know and offers to connect the prospect with you. No fake answers, no friction.

**Q4: "How is this different from hiring a part-time admin?"**
- A: A part-time admin costs $1,500–$2,500/month and works 9–5. The AI costs $25/month and works 24/7. An admin will eventually leave, get sick, or go on vacation. The AI doesn't. An admin can handle ~30 inquiries/month; the AI handles unlimited. Start with the AI; hire people later when you need strategic help, not inquiry handling.

**Q5: "What if I need to change how the AI answers questions?"**
- A: You tell David what needs updating in your monthly check-in. He retrains the AI—takes 1–2 days. New service? New pricing? New availability? David updates it and you're live again. This is included in the $25/month fee.

**Q6: "Can the AI work with [my specific CRM/calendar]?"**
- A: Probably. We integrate with 50+ CRMs and calendars (HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Zoho, Google Calendar, Calendly, Acuity, etc.). During discovery, you'll tell David what you use and we'll set it up. If something isn't supported, we find a workaround using Zapier or a custom integration.

**Q7: "What happens if I outgrow this and want a bigger system?"**
- A: You upgrade to the Presence or Growth tier. All your training, integrations, and conversation data move with you. You're not starting over—you're just adding more capabilities (website redesign, email marketing, content strategy, etc.). The AI stays and gets smarter.

## Content Tone & Voice Guidelines

- **Specificity over hype**: Use dollar amounts, percentages, real time frames. "Gets responses 10x faster" → "Response time drops from 6–24 hours to 5 seconds"
- **Founder voice**: Write like David—direct, honest, no sales jargon. Acknowledge trade-offs ("It won't replace you; it handles the routine stuff")
- **Problem-first**: Lead with the pain point, then the solution
- **Examples > abstractions**: Use the painting contractor example throughout; make it tangible
- **No false claims**: If the AI can't do something, say so and explain why (or what it CAN do instead)
